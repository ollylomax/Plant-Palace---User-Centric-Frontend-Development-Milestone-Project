A Journey from a Back Yard...

  • Jun 2017
    Inception

    After buying and selling some plant clippings on eBay and realising a potential market, aswell as gaining necessary knowledge and experience, the Plant Palace business idea was formed.

  • Aug 2018
    Home Growing

    We started to plant various species in batches in our back yard whilst continuing the buy-to-sell model in the background. Our knowledge and experience grows exponentially.

  • Jan 2020
    Productivity

    Our empire begins! After careful negotiations with distributors and vendord across the globe, we are able to drastically increase our logistical and warehousing capabilities and learn about many new species.

  • Oct 2021
    Shop Launch

    We launched our little store in downtown Plantington offering our full plant range and staff at hand to offer any advice and answer any queries you have. We expanded the store just last year to increase our stock volume.
