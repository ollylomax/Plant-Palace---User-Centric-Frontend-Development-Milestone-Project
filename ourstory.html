A Journey from a Back Yard...

  • Jun 2017

    Inception

    After buying and selling some plant clippings on eBay and realising a potential market, aswell as gaining necessary knowledge and experience, the Plant Palace business idea was formed.

  • Aug 2018

    Home Growing

    We started to plant various species in batches in our back yard whilst continuing the buy-to-sell model in the background. Our knowledge and experience grows exponentially.

  • Jan 2020

    Productivity

    Our empire begins! After careful negotiations with distributors and vendord across the globe, we are able to drastically increase our logistical and warehousing capabilities and learn about many new species.

  • Oct 2021

    Shop Launch

    We launched our little store in downtown Plantington offering our full plant range and staff at hand to offer any advice and answer any queries you have. We expanded the store just last year to increase our stock volume.

From Seed to Plant

Our Story

Tim Leafy and Jasmine Lovesafern started growing plants together 30 years ago in their back yard. Having originally met at university studying Botany and graduating together, they moved quickly in their relationship and married in 2001. Their love for nature and plants grew over time, eventually deciding to start and grow a business in order to monetize their shared enthusiasm for plants. It was such a roaring success that they both had to give up their jobs to focus fully on Plant Palace. They are still together to this day and still love to work in the York store twice a week. They would both love you to come and say hi!

As Plant Palace became more and more successful, we moved from our back yard into a large greenhouse on the outskirts of Huddersfield in order to rely less on our suppliers in 2007. It wasn't quite the Eden Project yet, but we expanded to a further three more large conservatories in 2010, at Sheffield and Rotherham. As of today, we have more than ten large greenhouses across the country and not only supply our own store and service our own mail orders, but also provide a service to local charities, churches and other local businesses. In 2022 we plan on launching our ecommerce platform and expand into the global market.

Our flagship store is located in the lovely and historic city of York, a cathedral city at the confluence of the rivers Ouse and Foss. The city has long-standing buildings and structures, such as a minster, castle and ancient city walls. It is the head city of historic Yorkshire and was a county corporate, outside of the county's council and the ridings. York is bounded by a green belt area, which more rigorously manages development around the rural area of the district and portions of surrounding districts, primarily to preserve the setting and historic character of York. The belt covers nearly all of the city district including all its outer villages, and extends into the surrounding North Yorkshire districts.
